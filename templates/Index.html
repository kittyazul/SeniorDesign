Welcome to PHISHING GUARD.

According to Enjoy Safer Technology (ESET) 97% of people are unable to identify sophisticated phishing attacks. Though phishing attempts use scare tactics, website forgeries, or scams to increase their effectiveness and chances of success, research shows that the heart of the problem resides in a lack of effective training. Timing is crucial for detecting and stopping phishing attacks.

Designed by Katie Drury, Sarah Matevia, and Serge Kikonda, Phishing Guard is a FREE, comprehensive, and easy to navigate web application designed to provide its users with the ability to receive interactive and effective training. Our goal is to ensure that the features within this application are tailored to you, our users, in such a way that you enjoy and easily retain what you learn, but also feel the need to learn more

In addition to creating a new approach to learning about phishing, this application will revolutionize the way these attacks are prevented. Phishing Guard has an easy to use menu system that includes short interactive quizzes, up to date videos, games, and more. These resources allow Phishing Guard to stand out among its peers, as it not only effectively decreases the success rate of phishing attempts but also increases user retention and awareness rate.


Hope you enjoy this journey,

Phishing Guard Team.
